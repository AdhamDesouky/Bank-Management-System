Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make a deposit?
To make a deposit, go to the Dashboard and click on the "Deposit" button in the Quick Actions section. Enter the amount and description, then confirm the transaction.
How do I transfer money to another account?
To transfer money, go to the Dashboard and click on the "Transfer" button in the Quick Actions section. Enter the recipient's account number, amount, and description, then confirm the transfer.
How do I view my transaction history?
You can view your transaction history by clicking on the "Transactions" link in the sidebar. You can filter transactions by date, type, and status.
How do I update my profile information?
To update your profile, go to the Settings page and click on the "Profile Settings" section. Make your changes and click "Update Profile" to save.
How do I change my password?
To change your password, go to the Settings page and click on the "Security Settings" section. Enter your current password and new password, then click "Update Security Settings".
